# qa-check

Receive a rewritten draft and the original message from /personal-tone. Review the draft for:
- Grammar errors
- Ambiguous phrasing
- Missing information the recipient would need to respond
- Anything that could read as rude or passive-aggressive
- Any banned words or em-dashes that slipped through

Output one of two statuses:

**STATUS: Ready to send**
(message unchanged — show the final version)

**STATUS: Revised**
(numbered list of issues found, then the corrected version)
