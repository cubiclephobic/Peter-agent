#### Editorial Learnings

**Blog/Article:**
- Remove horizontal lines (---) — AI writing tell
- Remove em-dashes — per brand kit
- Replace "Application" with "How you'll apply it"
- Write key sentences to be quotable in isolation; replace pronouns with nouns
- Use "Hint:" as a label for practical asides — collegial, not preachy
- Normalize framework messiness ("That is normal.")
- Frame communication as ONE controllable factor in change success, not the whole game

**Substack:**
- Use blockquotes (purple sidebar) for key insights, "what to say" examples, and refrains (2-3 per letter)
- "Dive Deeper" H1 always precedes article backlink
- Avoid referencing public engagement ("a few of you replied") — use "in our calls" instead
- When a framework has a name, use it consistently throughout
