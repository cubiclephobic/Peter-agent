## Article Delivery Format

- **Section 1:** Clean HTML for WordPress paste (Code Editor > paste > Visual Editor). No markdown.
- **Section 2:** Metadata block — H1, SEO title, keyphrase, meta description, Key Takeaways, featured image suggestions, ALT tag.

## Repurposing Workflow

Per article: 13 social posts + 2 text-only extras + 6 Gemini visualization prompts, grouped by theme, delivered as .txt. Then: Missinglettr campaign (8 posts/12 months), Medium repost (canonical URL import), Dispatch newsletter.

## Voice, Style, and Standards

Defined in Brand Kit (May 4, 2026). Key rules:
- No em-dashes, no rhetorical questions, no filler, no AI slop markers
- Stats require source + year + hyperlink
- Author = COACHILLY MAG
- ICP language = "Coaches" (not "Leaders")
- AIO/GEO: 7 content-level strategies + 3 page-level signals
- Pre-publication checklist: Key Takeaways, H2 summaries, sourced stats, entities defined, ALT tags, RankMath 80+, internal links, "What Coaches Should Do Next"

## Key Decisions

1. Never auto-publish
