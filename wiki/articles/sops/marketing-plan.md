# Zaradigm Marketing Plan 2026

## Strategic Positioning

**Forte:** High-stakes communication, positioning/branding, and building leadership confidence that advances careers and creates high-performing teams.

**The Pivot:** Heavy promotion of price-sensitive, short-term coaching (high urgency, low tenure) has not worked. Creates short-term clients who come for a narrow goal and aren't growth-minded. The shift in 2024-2025: focus on B2B and group formats.

---

## 2026 Portfolio Priorities (Ranked)

1. **B2B first** — HiPe TC, CATALYST, Fractional CMO/Agency work
2. **B2C group offers second** — the/SHIFT, CATALYST, on-demand/self-serve courses
3. **1:1 coaching last** — highest rates but trades time; no additional network value for clients; reserve for a select handful

**Philosophy on 1:1:** In a tight job market with flattened hierarchies, clients grow not just through coaching but through the network they get access to. 1:1 must be contained.

## Buyer's Journey — Leadership Coaching (Active Focus)

| Stage | Key Details |
|-------|-------------|
| **Awareness** | Goal: get promoted/become better communicator; Symptoms: insecurity, stuck, frustration; Where they look: YouTube, LinkedIn, courses, books; Risk of inaction: stuck career, potential demotion; Misconceptions: title = authority, knowing = mastering, leaders shouldn't need help |
| **Consideration** | Seeks: "know thyself" first, coach's experience, "program" structure; How they choose: program addresses specific pain points (communicate with executives, overcome impostor), dates fit schedule; Search terms: leadership coach + location, executive coach, "improve executive communication", "what skills do leaders need" |
| **Decision** | Criteria: program clarity, coach competence, career background; Objections: length, price; Convincing: core problem solved (sell the dream, not the service), trust, payment options |
