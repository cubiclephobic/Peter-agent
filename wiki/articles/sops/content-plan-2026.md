## Substack Weekly Letter — Four Types (Flexible Rotation)

Substack's purpose: leaders get the insider view from someone spending hundreds of hours in coaching rooms. Value = knowing they're not alone, seeing patterns, insight rooted in real organizational life.

| Type | Description | Length |
|------|-------------|--------|
| **Observation Letter** | ONE pattern heard repeatedly in coaching; personal opening ("This week, three leaders asked me…"); unpack what's really beneath the surface; why it matters now; what leaders get wrong | 500–700 words |
| **Framework Explainer** | ONE framework from coaching practice; explain "why" before "how"; real anonymized application; common mistakes | 600–800 words |
| **Challenge Letter** | Directly name an assumption or blind spot; why it's costly; what leaders think is true vs. what's actually true; the shift | 500–600 words |
| **Trending/Impact Letter** | Something in business/tech/culture shifting baseline expectations; how it shows up in client rooms; why leaders can't ignore it | 600–800 words |

### The Repurposing Workflow (45 Minutes Total)

1. Identify a source article
2. Answer discovery questions: What sparked this piece? Has thinking shifted? What feedback have leaders given? Which part comes up most in coaching? What do leaders miss? What would you say over coffee?
3. Identify 1-2 letter seeds (which type, what angle)
4. Draft in Corinna's voice (personal, conversational, insider-y)
5. Review (does it feel true? does it land?)
6. Add backlink naturally at end ("This connects to a longer piece I wrote on [topic] — full framework here if you want to go deeper")

**Timing:** You answer questions (20 min) → Draft (15 min) → Review/refine (10 min)

## How to Use the Evergreen Topics

- Pick 1-2 topics per month; rotate through pillars (don't do all "Clarity" back-to-back)
- Use the specific "angle" to guide article focus
- Each article naturally pulls case examples from coaching experience
- **Example rotation:** Feb: Pillar 1 + Pillar 4 → Mar: Pillar 2 + Pillar 5 → Apr: Pillar 3 + Pillar 6
