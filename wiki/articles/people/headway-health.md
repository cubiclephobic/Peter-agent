## Business Concept

**Phase One:** Mobile urgent care delivered via gig-based model — home visits, cash-pay, Florida market.

- Gig contractors (nurses/medics per visit) + doctor on-call via telehealth platform
- Owners do not need medical licenses
- Target: Florida elderly population (21%+ age 65+ now; growing to ~30% by 2045)
- White space confirmed: mobile urgent care is underserved vs. saturated IV therapy market

**Phase Two+ (not in current plan):** IV therapy, pharmacy delivery
