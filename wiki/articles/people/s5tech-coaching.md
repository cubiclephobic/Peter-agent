## Debrief Prep Framework

**Sequence:** Archetypes (top 1-3, common thread) → Traits (extremes only, flag tensions) → Facets (2-3 priority traits, surfaces the why) → "You in Context" (practical coaching hooks) → Synthesize 3-4 talking points (strength, friction, coaching question)

**Session flow (45 min):** Open/context-set (5 min) → Archetypes (10 min) → Traits & Facets (20 min) → Growth & Application (7 min) → Close (3 min)

**Ground rules:** High/low = patterns not judgments. Growth = behavior awareness, not personality change.

## Output Style Preferences (Apply to All S5Tech Work)

- Brief, structured, skimmable; synthesize patterns, not score lists
- Tone: warm, direct, empathic — not clinical
- Format: headers and bold for structure; brief enough for real-time session use
- No jargon; flag contradictions explicitly
- Archetypes as narrative first, then layer traits
- Growth-edge framing: positive and behavioral, never pathologizing
