# HiPe Team Coaching (HiPe TC)

## What It Is

HiPe TC is Zaradigm's team coaching offer. It uses a **solution-focused approach**, **team assessments**, and **business-mediation experience** to help teams function better — not by training individuals, but by working with the team as a system.

The name HiPe = High Performance. The offer is designed to create the conditions for high-performance team dynamics, not just teach communication skills in isolation.

---

## Who It's For

- **Leadership teams and project teams** navigating growth, change, or conflict
- **SMBs and mid-sized organizations** — typically 5–20 leaders per engagement
- Teams that are "one conflict away from formal mediation" — functional enough to still be working, dysfunctional enough that friction is visibly costing them
- Organizations where the team has capability individually but struggles to operate cohesively together

---

## Problems It Solves

- Conflict and friction between team members or functions
- Communication breakdowns (misalignment, avoidance, repeated misunderstandings)
- Slow decision-making — decisions made in circles, escalated unnecessarily, or delayed
- Change fatigue — team unable to adapt quickly enough during transitions
- Low trust, siloed working, reluctance to give direct feedback

---

## Outcomes Delivered

- Shared understanding of how the team operates (using assessment data as a mirror)
- Practical working agreements — co-created by the team, not imposed
- Better conversations: more direct, less defensive, less time wasted
- Faster decisions: clearer ownership, less revisiting
- Stronger trust: built through facilitated honesty, not just "team bonding"
- Neutral facilitation from a mediator-trained coach — holds space for real tension, not just the sanitized version

## Three Formats

### Online Workshop (1-Day)

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Price** | $11,000 up to 10 leaders; $1,100/additional leader (up to 20) |
| **Format** | Virtual, 1 full day |
| **Pre-work** | Team assessments included |
| **Includes** | 1–3 sponsor calls, session documentation, 1 follow-up session |
| **Upsell** | Annual assessment license: $200/person/year |
| **Best for** | Teams that want a data-driven reset without travel overhead |

### Onsite Workshop (1-Day)

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Price** | $15,000 up to 10 leaders; $1,500/additional leader |
| **Format** | In-person, 1 full day |
| **Pre-work** | Same as online — team assessments |
| **Includes** | Same content as online + in-room connection dynamic |
| **Extras** | Travel and on-site expenses billed separately; client handles venue |
| **Best for** | Teams who want the same impact as online + the energy of being in the room together |

### Transformation (6-Month)

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Price** | Multi-five-figure; ~$144,000 for a full-size strategic team engagement |
| **Format** | Weekly sessions, 60–90 min each; 6 months |
| **Pricing** | Customized by scope — team size, complexity, integration requirements |
| **Best for** | Teams with long-standing patterns that require sustained work to shift; high-stakes strategic teams |

---

## How to Choose a Format

| If… | Choose… |
|-----|---------|
| You want a fast, data-informed team reset without travel | Online Workshop |
| You want the same impact with the added energy of being in the room | Onsite Workshop |
| The team has deep, long-standing patterns that require sustained change | Transformation |
