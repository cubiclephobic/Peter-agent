# Mediation — Business Conflict Resolution

## What It Is

Business mediation is a per-engagement conflict resolution service under **Zaradigm**. Corinna is a certified business mediator. This is a distinct delivery mode from coaching and consulting — neutral facilitation between parties, not advisory or coaching.

**Brand:** Zaradigm
**Model:** Per-engagement
**Buyer:** C-suite, HR leaders, business owners (see [[avatar-profiles]] — ICP #1, crisis/conflict focus)

## How Mediation Fits the Zaradigm Ecosystem

From the Brand Portfolio Map:
- **Crisis/Conflict pillar:** Mediation handles case-specific resolution after the/SHIFT and CATALYST frameworks have been applied (or when conflict is too acute for a coaching approach)
- **Resilience pillar:** Mediation = resilience + repair after conflict + learning to prevent repeat patterns
- Mediation is the only Zaradigm offer that is *not* a group or cohort model — it's always per-engagement, case-specific
