# CATALYST Project Master Summary

## What This Is

CATALYST is a leadership mastermind group developed under the Zaradigm brand, targeting small business owners and CEOs. It is built around a leadership communication framework using the CATALYST model. The group focuses on growth through peer learning, shared experience, and laser coaching — not solo problem-solving.

## Key Framework Elements

- **Core principle:** "If you're not grounded in who you are and how you lead, nothing above it holds"
- Leadership modeled as a flexible continuum, not fixed positions
- Participant mindset: committed to learning and growing, values peer insight and shared experience
- Content draws from the/SHIFT model where leadership topics overlap
- Session format target: ~10–15 min concept/insight input, remainder for laser coaching and masterminding

---

## Key Decisions & Open Questions

- **Meeting format revision needed:** Current structure is curriculum-based; goal is to make it open-entry (join at any time, no sequential curriculum dependency)
- **Teaching placement:** Corinna is questioning whether frontloading teaching causes participants to lean back; hypothesis is that starting with specific challenges, then closing with a concept as a "topper," may be more effective
- **Own funnel needed:** Corinna needs to build and activate her own outreach sequence independent of the agency
