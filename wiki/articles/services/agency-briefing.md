# Agency Briefing: Zaradigm Services Overview

## Positioning

**Tagline:** Zaradigm develops conflict-capable, influence-strong leaders and teams who perform under pressure.

---

## Services Overview Table

| Service | Buyer | Problem | Outcome |
|---------|-------|---------|---------|
| **the/SHIFT** | HR, L&D, team leads | Leaders lack practical communication and influence skills; training doesn't stick | Leaders who practice real scenarios, build confidence under pressure, shift behavior durably |
| **CATALYST** | HR, C-suite | Leadership conflict or dysfunction is disrupting team performance | Clarity on the conflict dynamics, actionable path to resolution or realignment |
| **Team Coaching (TC / HiPe TC)** | CEO, COO, HR, team leaders | Team friction, silos, slow decisions, change fatigue, trust breakdowns | Shared language, working agreements, faster decisions, stronger collaboration |
| **Mediation** | C-suite, HR, business owners | Formal or near-formal conflict between parties; legal/HR escalation risk | Neutral facilitation to a written resolution agreement |
| **Leadership Communication Program** | HR, L&D, business owners | Leaders can't communicate with clarity, credibility, or influence | Practical communication frameworks applied to real leadership challenges |

## Key Sales Themes — All Offers

- Leaders who practice under realistic conditions outperform those who only receive training
- Conflict avoidance costs organizations more than conflict resolution
- Communication breakdowns are the #1 source of team dysfunction — and the most fixable
- Zaradigm's mediator background sets it apart: facilitation is neutral, outcomes-focused, and legally informed
- Practice-first > teach-first: skill change requires repetition in safe conditions

## Sales Pitch Scripts

### HiPe TC — Version 1

"Most teams don't have a communication problem — they have a conflict-avoidance problem. They've learned to work around each other instead of with each other, and it shows up as slow decisions, repeated misalignments, and meetings that solve nothing. HiPe Team Coaching gives your team a structured reset: we use assessments, facilitated practice, and working agreements to shift how the team actually operates — not just how they talk about operating. The result is a team that can have hard conversations faster, make decisions with less friction, and stay aligned through change."

### HiPe TC — Version 2

"When a team is stuck — even a talented one — the root cause is almost always relational, not technical. They may be smart and capable individually, but they haven't developed the shared language, trust, or communication habits to function at full capacity together. I come in as a mediator-trained facilitator, not just a coach, which means I can hold space for real tension — not just the polished version of it. HiPe TC is built around a full-day intensive (online or onsite) that includes team assessments, scenario-based practice, and a set of working agreements the team actually writes together. Within two weeks of that session, most teams report faster decisions and fewer unspoken standoffs."

### the/SHIFT — Version 1

"Most leadership development fails because it's all input and no practice. Managers sit through workshops, nod at the frameworks, and return to Monday unchanged — because nothing made them actually try the thing in a high-stakes moment. the/SHIFT is different: it's a leadership practice lab. Every session, leaders work through real scenarios they're currently navigating — difficult conversations, managing up, influencing without authority — and they practice responses, get feedback, and build the muscle. Six months of bi-weekly sessions means cumulative skill, not a one-time event."

### the/SHIFT — Version 2

"The leaders you're investing in are probably smart, motivated, and already good at their jobs. What they're missing isn't knowledge — it's practice under pressure. the/SHIFT is structured leadership practice: bi-weekly sessions where participants work through real, messy leadership scenarios in a safe environment, coached in the moment, building patterns that transfer to the actual workplace. It's designed for companies who want their emerging leaders to grow into senior roles with fewer growing pains — and for senior leaders who need to sharpen their influence and communication at the executive level."
