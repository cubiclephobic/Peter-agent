## The SHIFT Leadership Model

- Self-awareness and contextual awareness working together
- Leadership as a flexible continuum — not fixed positions
- People move up/down based on life circumstances (stress, change, uncertainty, growth)
- Attention shifts with people's needs
- Core grounding principle: "If you're not grounded in who you are and how you lead, nothing above it holds"
- Complete arc: **model → reasoning → dynamic application → why it rests on the core**
