## What VAS Means

**VAS = Value-Added Services.** CMAG's partner referral pages — each is a dedicated page on coachilly.com that promotes a partner's service relevant to coaches. Revenue model is referral/profit share. Pages are referral hubs, not storefronts.

VAS CTA boxes appear on all 57 CMAG content pages, linking to active VAS pages.
