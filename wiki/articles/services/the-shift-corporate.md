# the/SHIFT — Corporate Program Details

## What It Is

A leadership communication lab for emerging and mid-level leaders who need to turn teams around through better conversations, not more meetings. Gives leaders a safe, structured space to work on the real conversations that define their careers and the business: high-stakes decisions, conflict, performance, and change.

---

## Who It's For

- Leaders who can't get teams aligned because their communication is unclear or inconsistent
- Teams stuck in confusion, passive resistance, or internal politics
- Managers who avoid or mishandle tough conversations (performance, conflict, change)
- High-potential leaders not yet ready for bigger responsibility due to communication gaps
- Managers who recently transitioned from IC to people leadership or became "leader of leaders"

---

## Problems Solved

- Strategy and priorities clear on paper but lost as they move through layers of leaders and teams
- High-stakes initiatives slow down because leaders avoid or mishandle tough conversations
- Escalations, rework, and people issues consume senior leadership time that should be on strategy
- Talent risk: strong ICs promoted to leadership without the communication skills to lead people
- Organizational politics and "shadow conversations" erode trust and kill honest input

---

## Outcomes (After 6 Months)

- Communicate more clearly and credibly in high-stakes situations
- Set expectations and boundaries without aggression or avoidance
- Handle conflict in ways that build, not break, trust
- Navigate organizational politics more intelligently and ethically
- Turn everyday leadership moments (1:1s, team meetings, stakeholder updates) into alignment opportunities

---

## Program Format

- **Duration:** 6 months
- **Cadence:** Bi-weekly 90-minute live online labs
- **Group size:** Up to 10 leaders in a private corporate cohort (option to add more)
- **Between sessions:** Reflection prompts and hands-on action items applied directly to current conversations and challenges

---

## What Happens in a Lab

Each session combines:
- Short, practical input on a leadership communication theme (executive presence, managing up, handling resistance)
- Practice using concrete tools such as the Leader's User Manual (LUM)
- Red-Team-style peer coaching: "What if…?", Five Whys, perspective-shifting
- Live practice and feedback in a psychologically safe environment

**Key focus areas:**
- Executive presence — how leaders show up in rooms that matter
- Leader's User Manual (LUM) — making each leader's expectations and working style explicit and readable
- Managing up, across, and down — communicating effectively in all directions
- Handling tough conversations — conflict, resistance, performance, and change
- Navigating power dynamics and organizational culture

---

## Corporate Cohort — Included / Not Included

**Included:**
- 2-4 sponsor calls (with HR/exec sponsor) to clarify context, define success, align on participants and timing
- Tailoring of selected labs, examples, and practice conversations to the organization's environment
- Periodic check-ins with sponsor to review progress and make targeted adjustments

**Not included:**
- 1:1 leadership coaching for individual participants (available as separate upgrade)
- Any in-person elements (travel, on-site meetings, venues)
- Internal logistics and communication inside the organization

---

## Is the/SHIFT Right for You?

Fit if:
- You have 6-12 emerging or mid-level leaders who are critical to execution and change
- You want sustained behavior change over 6 months, not a one-off training
- You're prepared to partner on context and success criteria, not just "send people to a course"

If the main need is a short, time-bound reset for a single team → HiPe TC workshop or SPRINT may be a better first step.
