# Domain — Field, Industry, and Area of Expertise

## The Short Version

**Zaradigm develops conflict-capable, influence-strong leaders and teams who perform under pressure.**

The work sits at the intersection of three disciplines that most practitioners treat separately: leadership communication, team coaching, and business mediation. That combination is the differentiator.

---

## Primary Disciplines

### 1. Leadership Communication

The core. Everything Zaradigm does traces back to communication as the foundational leadership skill. Not public speaking or presentation polish — the everyday, high-stakes conversations that define careers and organizations: managing up, navigating conflict, influencing without authority, leading through change.

**What makes it different from generic "communication coaching":**
- Tied to real organizational outcomes (promotions, stakeholder alignment, team performance)
- Practice-first, not theory-first — skill requires reps, not just frameworks
- Addresses the whole system: the leader's own patterns + the political and relational environment they're operating in

### 2. High-Performance Team Coaching

Working with teams as a system, not just coaching individuals. Uses:
- **Solution Focused Team Coaching® framework** — starts with what's working, not what's broken
- **PrinciplesUs™ / PrinciplesYou™ assessments** — personality and team assessments as a shared mirror
- **Business mediation experience** — can hold space for real conflict, not just polished disagreement

**Named methodology:** HiPe Team Communication™

### 3. Business Mediation

Neutral facilitation between parties in business disputes. Certified through UTD (University of Texas at Dallas). Governed by Texas law (Tex. Civ. Prac. & Rem. Code § 154.053).

**Why it matters beyond standalone mediation engagements:** The mediation credential shapes the coaching and team work. Corinna can facilitate sessions where real stakes and emotions are high. That's rare in the coaching/L&D space. No competitor in the leadership communication space brings this.
